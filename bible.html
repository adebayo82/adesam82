CHOOSE FRIENDSHIPS WISELY

BIBLE PRINCIPLE: "Bad associations corrupt
good morals."-1 Corinthians 15:33.

WHAT IT MEANS: The people you spend time with
have a great influence on you-for good or for
bad. Sociologists agree that such influence can
affect your life. Foe example, they say that if you
surround yourself with people who smoke or who
are getting divorced, you may be more likely to
start smoking or to want a divorce.

WHAT YOU CAN DO: Make friends with people
who display qualities and have values you admire
or want to imitate. For instance, seek association
with people who are tactful, respectful, generous,
and hospitable.
